Zee’s popular show Sapne Suhane Ladakpan Ke is soon going to bid adieu to its viewers. And the buzz is that Zee is launching a new show Hello Pratibha in place of that with an intent to capture attention of housewives and women in general .Story revolves around a neglected housewife Pratibha who does it all for her family yet is not given her due credit.It showcases struggle of a housewife for finding her own identity. Binny Sharma ,the actress who was last seen in Zee’s show Sangini and is popular from her dance reality show Dance India Dance 2 appearance will be essaying the title role. Show will be produced by Shakuntalam Telefilms and also features Sachal Tyagi of ‘Agle Janam Mohe Bitya ‘ fame as Pratibha’s husband.

So lets welcome Pratibha and watch how Pratibha explores herself and finds her worth in her journey!

Rashmi Sharma Productions will be making a long running typical desi masala soap for Zee TV channel and we hear the show is in its budding stages, and the casting process is presently on.
But seems like the main lead has already been decided and its none other than Harshad Arora of Beintehaa fame, who will grace our TV screens in a contrasting character when compared to his role of Zain in Beintehaa.
And buzz also is that the female lead has also been decided, Shiny Doshi, who essayed the role of Kumud’s sister, Kusum in Saraswatichandra. Though the beautiful actor remained out of reach for any comment.
As for Harshad, he said “Yes, talks are certainly on. All I want to tell you now is that this will be a very different character, totally unlike Zain and Harshad. If I get this, it will be a new challenge in terms of the role for me.”
From the sources we cam to know that the lead cast has been locked, and the pilot for the show will be shot soon. The show is expected to roll fast, as the channel is readying few new launches in the first quarter of the year.
