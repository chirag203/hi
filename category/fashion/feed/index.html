Hollywood actress Anjanaa Bhattacharya recently shot with French photographer Nosh R Mather for a concept shoot for an international fashion magazine. Nosh R Mather is a French photographer who has worked with international ad agencies and clients across the world.

He graduated from the Sir JJ school of Arts in Mumbai with a post graduation majoring in photography from the Young School of Communications, Paris. He has been associated with the top three ad agencies in the World for over a decade as a Creative Director and a people and fashion photographer for various international fashion magazines and has been associated with LOWE, JWT, INTERADSand O&M internationally for a span of 13 years according to an IndyaNewz.com report.

Award-winning Kathak dancer, actress and model Anjanaa Bhattacharya also shot with designer label brand Randell Gomes recently for an Indo-western fusion and pret range as well as a hand-embroidered and embellished sari and Anarkali collection. The new Spring collection will be launched from Randell Gomes’ boutique in in South Goa. The Randell Gomes Chikankari Sari Collection as well as the Chikankari Anarkali Collection incorporates intricate hand-work and embroidery including about 22 different styles or types of hand-embroidered chikankari stich-work.

Randell Gomes has also launched a line of Chikankari Anakarlis. Chikankari is delicate and artfully done hand embroidery on a variety of textile fabrics like muslin, silk, chiffon, organza, net etc. White thread is embroidered on cool, pastel shades of light muslin and cotton garments. Currently, chikan embroidery is also done with coloured and silk threads in different colours to sync with recent fashion trends and keep chikankari trendy with changing fashion and fabric according to IndyaNewz.com.

Anjanaa Bhattacharya who has been signed on by Hollywood director David S Wolfe for a crossover film Love & Passion, has also featured in films like Zilla Gaziabad, Right Ya Wrong, Chashme Baddoor and has done ad-films like Vithoba Dantmanjan, Nmart, Zalim lotion, Kabjamrut, etc. She also did a Bhojpuri film Ee Mati Mein Sab Kuch Kathe produced by her Guruji Jayanti Mala Mishra – daughter of the legendary Kathak dancer Sitara Devi – in which she played the lead female role. She has also been doing theatre for the last 5 years and has acted in plays like Uncle Samjha Karo, See No Evil Hear No Evil and Get rid of my Wife. She has also done several Tamil and Telugu films in the past.
